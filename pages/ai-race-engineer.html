Project Overview

An AI-powered race engineer for the F1 games.

code

Background

While playing the F1 video game, the player needs to access and keep track of various kinds of data while focusing on driving. This requires the player to press buttons and navigate menus while driving. To solve this I created an AI race engineer. The AI race engineer simulates the real-life role of a race engineer who communicates with the driver during a race. In both the game and in the real world, telemetry data is constantly generated. This data is expansive and covers information such as tire and brake temperatures, car damage, lap times, track conditions, and much more. The AI race engineer uses the telemetry data from the game to provide the player with real-time race updates and answers to their questions.
